Privacy Policy

We care about your focus — and your privacy

Short Version

Pomodoram.online collects absolutely no personal data from you.

No emails, no names, no IP addresses are stored on our servers. There are no accounts, no analytics tools, no cookies for tracking, no ads, and no third-party scripts that spy on your usage.

Everything happens right in your browser. Your timer settings (if you change them) are saved only locally using your browser's storage — we never see or receive them.

What We Collect (Nothing Personal)

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). This includes:

  • No email addresses
  • No names or usernames
  • No phone numbers or contact details
  • No location data
  • No browsing habits or usage statistics

The only "data" involved is JavaScript running in your browser to run the timer — nothing is sent anywhere.

Technical Details

Pomodoram.online is a completely client-side web application:

  • All timer logic, sounds, and settings are handled in your browser
  • LocalStorage may be used (only by you, on your device) if you customize durations or themes
  • No server-side logging or databases are involved
  • No Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Hotjar, or any tracking service is loaded
  • No ads or affiliate links

Because of this minimal approach, there is no personal data to protect, share, or delete.

Third Parties

We do not use any third-party services that collect your data.

If your browser automatically sends basic HTTP information (like IP address) to our hosting provider when you visit the site, that is standard web behavior and outside our control — we do not store or use it in any way.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever add features that collect any kind of data (which is very unlikely), we will update this page and clearly explain the changes.
