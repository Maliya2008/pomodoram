My Story

Hi, I'm Malindu — just a student who used to get distracted way too easily while studying.

I tried lots of timers and productivity apps, but most were too complicated — too many features, logins, notifications, ads… I just wanted something clean, fast, and actually useful every day.

So I created Pomodoram.online the way I needed it: no account, no tracking, no clutter. Just work 25 minutes → break 5 minutes → repeat. Simple as that.

Now I’m happy to share it with other students, remote workers, and anyone who wants better focus without the extra noise.

What We Stand For

Focus should be simple. Pomodoram follows the classic Pomodoro technique but removes everything unnecessary.

No sign-ups, no subscriptions, no overwhelming settings — just a beautiful, lightweight timer that helps you stay in the zone.
